## 🚨 If They Say No - Your Options

### Option 1: Stripe Dispute
1. Go to cloud.browser-use.com/manage
2. Access your Stripe customer portal
3. Find the charges for Browser Use
4. Click "Dispute" or "Report Issue"
5. Explain: "Minimal usage, requested refund, was denied"

### Option 2: Credit Card Chargeback
1. Call your credit card company
2. Say: "I want to dispute charges from Browser Use"
3. Explain: Minimal usage, subscription you barely used, refund denied
4. Timeline: You usually have 60-120 days from charge date
5. Note: Your Feb 25, Jan 25, Dec 25 charges may still be within this window

### Option 3: Small Claims Court
- For $90, probably not worth it
- But you could mention this possibility in escalation

### Option 4: Public Review
- Leave honest review on Reddit, Trustpilot, etc.
- Many companies respond when issues go public
- Keep it factual, not emotional
